Unplanned trip to Alibaug

5th March, 2020


This was planned around Holi festival in 2020, it was a casual day we came back from college it was friday night me and my friends met for dinner and out of nowhere we started talking hypothetically let's go somewhere, it was me and my rommate on the same night we surfed around internet for nearby locations and there Alibaug poped up. We googled and came to a website thrillophilia and we got a wonderfull plan for 2N/1D camping in front of beach. Me and my roomate got overexcited and booked.

Day 1: We woke up at 7 because of the excitement of trip we travelled from pune to Alibaug it took around 4-5 hours. We reached the campsite, They welcomed us with evening snacks and after that we went to beach and enjoyed the beautiful sunset. Later that night trip organise arranged amazing dinner with wide variety of food we were wide awake till 2-3AM listening to music enjoying pleasant voice of waves.

Day 2: We woke up early enjoyed the sunrise with the beach had breakfast and headed for our next detination where we had amazing sports ride. It was very calm and pleasant enviornment. We booked a resort for the next day reached there had our lunch and took a power nap. After that went for the beach watching the sunset. Again spent the whole evening till night around the beach as it was so calm and pleasant why would anyone leave? But yes trip was going to end and the very next day early morning we headed to Pune.
